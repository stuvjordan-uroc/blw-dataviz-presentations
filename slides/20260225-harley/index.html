Democracy = voting?

Democracy: A political system in which the likelihood that a government officeholder keeps her job depends on whether a majority of the persons she governs approve of her performance.

Elected politicians hate democracy.

Every free, fair, and competitive election should be the last.

Free, Fair, and Competitive Election: An election in which almost everyone has the right to vote, most people do vote, every vote counts equally, and there are multiple candidates all of whom have a decent chance of winning.
